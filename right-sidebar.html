Behold the FAQ

Where your curious questions are put to rest.

How do I sign up?

There is a sign in button on this navbar if you prefer to log in with a Google account. Otherwise, there is a sign up button on the home page navbar, which takes you to a part of the site where you can proceed to create your own account. When you create your account, don't forget to verify your email address. After, explore the website!

How do I submit?

The post button on the navbar takes you to a page where you can submit your ideas. You don't have to be logged in at the moment to submit ideas. On the navbar of the idea submission page, there is a button that shows all the ideas that have been posted so far.
