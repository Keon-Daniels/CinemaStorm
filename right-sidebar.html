Behold the FAQ

Where your curious questions are put to rest.

How do I sign up?

There is a sign in button on this navbar if you prefer to log in with a Google account. Otherwise, there is a sign up button on the home page navbar, which takes you to a part of the site where you can proceed to create your own account. When you create your account, don't forget to verify your email address. After, explore the website!

How do I submit ideas?

The browse button on the navbar takes you to a page where you can submit your ideas. You don't have to be logged in at the moment to submit ideas. On the navbar of the idea submission page, there is a button that shows all the ideas that have been posted so far.

What happens if I get picked for the pitch competition?(Premium Version Only)

Once the Premium Version releases, the top 15 voted that can participate will be selected for a pitch competition. CinemaStorm will contact you for what you have to do in order for you to participate.

What does the free version entail?

The free version allows you and other people that like your idea to collaborate in making your own movie. You can submit your idea via the browse button, and people who upvote your idea can contact you through your email, and the movie can be est up and made. This helps a lot of people who have experience in any type of film making can help direct, flim, edit, and produce the movie.

What happens if I get picked for the pitch competition and don't want to participate?

That's fine, because you still have the option to create your own movie with the community.If you have the premium version, that just means that you are more willing to invest money into your movie idea, but that doesn't stop you from creating a movie with the people that like your idea.
